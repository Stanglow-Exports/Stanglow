Our Styles

Casual Printed Sarees

For those casual yet elegant moments, our printed sarees bring a touch of joy and vibrancy to your everyday style. Choose from a palette inspired by the vibrant colors of Rajasthan, Kerala, and Gujarat.

Cotton Sarees

Embrace comfort and style with our glamorous cotton sarees, a blend of simplicity and sophistication that's perfect for any occasion. Discover the charm of Chanderi, Tant, and Kota cotton sarees.

Traditional Silk Sarees

Experience the grandeur of traditional silk sarees, woven with intricate designs that pay homage to centuries of craftsmanship. Explore the rich heritage of Banarasi, Kanjivaram, and Mysore silk sarees.

Bandhej Sarees

Indulge in the mesmerizing art of tie-and-dye with our Bandhej sarees. Each piece is a masterpiece of the profound beauty that encapsulates the essence of Rajasthan's rich cultural heritage.

Bridal Sarees

Celebrate love and union with our exquisite bridal sarees, meticulously designed to make your special day truly magical. Adorn yourself in the regal elegance of Banarasi, Paithani, and Lehenga sarees.

Designer Embroidered Sarees

Unleash the true potential of your wardrobe by adorning yourself in our captivating designer embroidered sarees, where every stitch reflects creativity and contemporary flair. Indulge in the beauty of Lucknowi, Kantha, and Zardosi work.

Lehengas

Lehenga reflects opulence of indian bridal wear & festive attire. Each piece is designed to make a statement, adorned with intricate embroidery & rich fabrics.

Dress Materials

A range of high quality dress materials that allow customers to customize their outfits according to their preferences. These materials feature exquisite prints & embellishments.
